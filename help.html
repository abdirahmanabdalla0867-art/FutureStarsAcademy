Website Help Guide

🏠 Home Page

The Home page introduces Future Stars Football Academy. You can read about our academy, coaching programs, and football services.

🖼 Gallery Page

The Gallery page displays football training photos and a table showing available training programs, duration, and fees.

📝 Enrollment Page

Use the Enrollment page to register as a player. Complete the form by entering your personal information and click the Enroll button.

👤 About Page

The About page provides information about the academy, its mission, vision, values, and the project developer.

📞 Contact

For more information, contact the academy by email or phone using the details shown on the Home page.

💡 Tips

Use the navigation menu at the top of every page to move easily between pages. Make sure all required fields are completed before submitting the enrollment form.
